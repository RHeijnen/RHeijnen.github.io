Personas

Published: 01 Feb 2020

Personas are an important part of the development/design process. They are vital in answering questions like 'what motivates our users to use this product'.

Personas give you insight into your target audience, they could be continiously updated if your target audience fluctuates and/or market positions change.

A big pitfall of developing anything is that you are always biased, you are always working within the context of your own experiences and knowledge. Personas help you to put on a 'client cap' to see and even argue from their perspective when it comes to designs and implementations.

Take a look at these examples of personas and see if there is anything you can learn from these and apply to your own organisation / project.
