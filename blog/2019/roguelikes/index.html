Roguelikes.

Really is the hot new thing these days huh,

I remmember my brother actually playing Rogue when I was a kid, I never really got into it I was more into Donald Duck at the time, grinding money and upgrading my playground. But many years later I did get into rogue likes, and I never grew out of it.

One of the earliest roguelikes I remember playing and at the same time my favorite rogue like - I still play it frequently to this day. FTL: Faster Than Light - It is an amazing combination of randomness, strategy and firefighter/spaceship sim.

The great thing that makes me come back to FTL (besides the modding community) is its simplicity in its gameplay, there is the fighting scene, you see above - there is an alternative scene without an enemy space ship, and there is a map. Most of the content takes place inside these scenes with dialog windows and story line descisions.

Another thing I want to note, is the strategy part of FTL. This is a rare combination of gameplay - not many have tried and even less succeeded (looking at you pixel piracy).
